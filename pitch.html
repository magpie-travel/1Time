The Pitch

"Capture One Memory. Keep It Forever."

1. Executive Summary

1time.ai is a platform for capturing and sharing life's most viral moments. We use AI to help users create emotionally rich, shareable stories from recent events—concerts, games, weddings, and vacations. Our mission is to transform fleeting moments into lasting digital memories that strengthen social bonds.

While our platform also supports deep legacy preservation, our go-to-market strategy is focused on the immediate, shareable joy of collective memory, creating a powerful engine for viral growth and user acquisition.

2. The Problem

We experience amazing moments, but the stories behind them fade.

  • Photos aren't enough: A picture of a concert doesn't capture the roar of the crowd. A wedding photo doesn't include the funny speeches.
  • Memories are fragmented: Everyone at an event has a different perspective. The complete story is scattered across phones and minds.
  • Sharing is generic: Posting a photo to social media lacks the deep emotional context of the actual experience.
  • The moment is lost: Without a dedicated tool, the energy and emotion of a shared experience disappears almost instantly.

3. The Solution

1time.ai lets users and their friends co-create and share the story of an event, in their own voices.

Concerts & Sports: Relive the game-winning shot or the encore from every angle.
Weddings & Birthdays: Create a "living guestbook" of audio messages from everyone who was there.
College & School Moments: Settle old debates by compiling everyone's version of a legendary story.
Travel & Vacations: Build a collaborative travelogue with your friends or family as it happens.

4. Product Roadmap

✅ Core MVP

Create a shared "Memory Wall" for an event. Users can contribute text and voice notes. AI generates a highlight reel for easy sharing.

🔜 Phase 2

QR code invitations for live events. AI-powered "He Said, She Said" video templates that splice together different perspectives.

🎯 Phase 3

Branded Memory Walls for sponsors. Integration with ticketing platforms. Voice-cloning for personalized recaps.

6. Business Model

Viral Growth Fueled by Sponsorships and Freemium

Viral Freemium Model

Users can create and contribute to a limited number of memories for free. Power features like private vaults, voice cloning, and long-term storage are part of a premium subscription.

Sponsored Memory Campaigns

Brands (e.g., Expedia, Coca-Cola, sports leagues) sponsor Memory Walls at major events, gaining access to authentic user-generated content and a new channel for fan engagement.

7. Go-To-Market Strategy

🌀 Viral Product Hooks

Every memory is a shareable prompt. QR codes at events make it frictionless to join and contribute.

🎥 Creator Campaigns

Partnering with event and travel influencers to create Memory Walls for their followers at concerts, festivals, and trips.

🤝 B2B2C Partnerships

Integrating with wedding planners, event organizers, and venues to offer 1time.ai as a standard add-on.
