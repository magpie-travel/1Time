1time.ai

Capture One Memory. Keep It Forever.

Problem

  • We forget 60% of life events in a year.
  • We rarely preserve stories or voices.
  • Memories fade, distort, and vanish.
  • We have trackers for steps — but not our most meaningful moments.

Solution

1time.ai helps people speak or write their memories, then turns them into narrated, shareable stories — saved forever in their own voice.

Product Features

  • Memory capture (voice/text)
  • AI-generated storytelling
  • Voice narration (TTS & cloning)
  • Memory vaults and sharing
  • Time capsule delivery
  • Storybooks & tribute kits

Business Model

  • Free to start with 1 memory
  • Subscription tiers ($5, $15, $30/month)
  • Revenue from memory books, tributes, and QR kits
  • Sponsored moments from brands like Expedia, EPL, and Hallmark

Go-To-Market

  • Viral product loops (share/gift a memory)
  • Influencer storytelling campaigns
  • Sponsored event activations
  • Partnerships with weddings, funerals, and therapy providers

Vision

A billion memories, beautifully remembered — 1time.ai is the world’s emotional memory vault.

“We don’t want your memories to go viral. We want them to outlive you.”
