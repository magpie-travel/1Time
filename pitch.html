The Pitch

"Capture One Memory. Keep It Forever."

1. Executive Summary

1time.ai is a memory preservation platform powered by AI and voice synthesis. It allows users to capture personal stories and transform them into lasting digital memories — written, narrated, and emotionally rich. Our mission is to help people protect what matters most: the voices, stories, and relationships that define their lives.

We’re launching at the intersection of generative AI, digital legacy, and emotional storytelling — creating a brand-new consumer behavior around memory preservation and digital heirlooms.

2. The Problem

We forget, distort, and delay capturing our most meaningful memories.

  • Memory fade is rapid: 60% of life events are forgotten within a year.
  • Each recall rewrites the memory: making it emotional but less accurate.
  • We preserve photos, not stories: voices, context, and emotions are lost.
  • When people pass away, their stories go with them — forever.

3. The Solution

1time.ai enables users to save, narrate, and share real-life memories — in their own voice, or the voice of someone they love.

Voice journals for parents & families
Tributes to loved ones after death
Love stories & life milestones
Travel memories and digital time capsules

4. Product Roadmap

✅ Core MVP

Record a memory via text or voice, turn it into a narrative story with default AI narration, and share privately.

🔜 Phase 2

Clone your own voice, choose story themes (tribute, letter), and schedule memories for future delivery.

🎯 Phase 3

Premium narration styles, family tree story connections, and AI-powered conversations with memory archives.

6. Business Model

Subscription Tiers & Add-Ons

Subscriptions

A freemium model with tiered plans (Core, Voice+, Legacy) offering progressively powerful features like unlimited memories, voice cloning, and AI-narrated books.

Add-Ons & Partnerships

One-time purchases like printed memory books and QR memory cards for events. White-label bundles for partners in the funeral, wedding, and therapy industries.

7. Go-To-Market Strategy

🌀 Viral Hooks

Every memory is a shareable prompt, inviting new users to contribute their perspective.

🎥 Creator Campaigns

Partnering with influencers on TikTok and Instagram for authentic storytelling challenges.

🤝 B2B2C Partnerships

Integrating with funeral homes, wedding planners, and therapists to reach users at key life moments.
