Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after my free trial?

Your 50 memories will remain safe and accessible. To add more memories or use premium features, you'll be invited to upgrade to a paid plan. There's no automatic charge.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your annual subscription at any time. You will retain access to premium features until the end of your billing period. You will always have access to the memories you've saved.

Is the Legacy AI a one-time payment?

Yes. The $299 is a one-time setup and training fee for your AI persona. However, an active Legacy Premium subscription is required to continue adding new memories to keep your AI up-to-date.

How is my data kept private and secure?

Privacy is our highest priority. All your data is end-to-end encrypted. We never sell your data or use it for advertising. You are the sole owner of your memories. Please read our full Ethics Pledge for more details.
